ABOUT US

We Are Modern Innovative Custom Steel Fabrication Company

Getsee Design is a Custom Steel Fabrication Company based in Co. Mayo specialising in light engineering works. Our goal is to provide high quality products and service to both Industrial and Residential sectors.

As an Irish based Company we endeavor to source our materials locally. This ensures the quality of our material and supports the local economy.
